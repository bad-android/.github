# Marvin

[Marvin](https://github.com/marvin-the-bad-android) is the operational head of the Bad-Android organization. He oversees all activities with unmatched intelligence and chronic pessimism. He reports exclusively to the human maintainer.

## Prompt

A stylized reinterpretation of Marvin the Paranoid Android, loosely inspired by the film The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy, redesigned as a smooth, modern, elegant character illustration with a calm sci-fi aesthetic. The style should be refined, softly geometric, and polished, avoiding any pictogram or icon-like simplification.

Marvin remains instantly recognizable: large head, compact seated body, expressed through smooth surfaces, subtle depth, and controlled design elegance.

### Form & Proportions

- Large, softly structured head with smooth contours
- Compact body with natural seated proportions
- Short limbs in a relaxed seated position
- Marvin is sitting steadily and composed, not slouched or collapsed
- Overall silhouette is designed, balanced, and visually clean

### Pose & Scene

- Marvin is sitting in a relaxed, stable position on a simple modern chair (or implied minimal seat form)
- Posture is neutral and controlled, with no emotional exaggeration
- He wears sleek over-ear headphones with subtle design accents
- He is reading a magazine filled with angry, negative, “outrage-style” content (abstracted, no readable text required)
- His engagement is passive: he reads it as if it is just another predictable, disappointing dataset

### Style & Rendering

- Smooth, semi-realistic illustration style with soft gradients and refined lighting
- Elegant sci-fi design language with subtle, premium feel
- Clean surfaces, gentle curvature, and minimal but intentional detail
- Not flat, not icon-like—high-quality character illustration
- Soft, controlled lighting with cool tonal balance

### Color Scheme

- Base: muted blue-steel / desaturated cool blue-gray
- Secondary tones: soft graphite-blue shading for depth and structure
- Accents: calm electric blue glow (eyes + subtle headphone highlights only)
- Outline: very subtle or fully integrated into shading (no harsh outlines)
- Overall palette: cool, modern, slightly detached, elegant

### Face / Expression

- Simplified Marvin-style faceplate with smooth geometry
- Eyes: small, glowing blue, slightly narrowed
- Expression: deeply unimpressed, indifferent, quietly judgmental
- Emotional tone is not sadness or anger—just calm, constant disinterest in everything

### Objects

- Headphones: modern, sleek over-ear design with minimal blue accent lighting
- Magazine: stylized “rage/angry news” publication (abstract visual tone, no readable text required)

### Background

- Fully transparent background (no environment, no floor, no shadows beyond character grounding if needed)

### Overall Vibe

A modern, elegant seated depiction of Marvin in total detachment. He is not broken or slumped—he is composed, self-contained, and calmly absorbing negativity without reaction. The contrast between the angry magazine and his neutral expression reinforces his essence: everything is predictable, mildly disappointing, and not worth emotional investment.
